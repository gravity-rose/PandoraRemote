Control Pandora from your wrist. PandoraRemote lets you manage playback, rate songs, and adjust volume directly from your Pebble watch.

FEATURES

- Skip tracks, play/pause with a single button press
- Thumbs up or thumbs down the current song
- Adjust phone volume from your watch
- See the current song, artist, and station on your watch
- Auto-launches when Pandora starts playing
- Works on all Pebble hardware (Time, Time Steel, Time Round, Pebble 2, Time 2, Core 2 Duo, Core Time)

HOW IT WORKS

PandoraRemote has three screens you cycle through with the select button:

Playback: Skip to the next track or play/pause.
Rating: Thumbs up or thumbs down the current song.
Volume: Turn the volume up or down on your phone.

SETUP

1. Install the PandoraRemote companion app on your Android phone.
2. Grant notification access to PandoraRemote in your phone's settings.
3. Start Pandora — the watch app launches automatically.
